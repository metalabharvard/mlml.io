Robert Gerard Pietrusko is an assistant professor in the departments of Landscape Architecture and Architecture at Harvard's Graduate School of Design, where his teaching and research focuses on geospatial representation, simulation, narrative + critical cartography, and spatial taxonomies. His design work has been exhibited at the MoMA, SFMOMA, The Foundation Cartier, and the Venice Architecture Biennale, among others, and has been featured in Metropolis, Architectural Record, and Domus. In 2011, he was an artist-in-residence at the ZKM Center for Art and Media in Karlsruhe, Germany, and at the Experimental Media and Performing Arts Center (EMPAC) in Troy, NY.
